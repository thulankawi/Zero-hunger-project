Targets for make a no hunger world


Sustainable food production

target aims to ensure that food is produced in a way that meets current demands without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It involves practices that are environmentally sustainable, such as efficient water use, minimal use of pesticides, and soil conservation.This will help the future generations to eliminate hunger by creating the necessary environment for it.

Food safety

This target focuses on ensuring that all food throughout the supply chain, from production to consumption, is safe for human consumption. This involves preventing contamination, proper handling, storage, and distribution practices, and ensuring that foodborne illnesses are minimized.

Quality food production without chemicals

This target emphasizes producing food without excessive or harmful chemical inputs. It promotes organic farming practices, integrated pest management, and sustainable agriculture techniques that maintain soil fertility and biodiversity while minimizing the use of synthetic chemicals.So this'll help to eliminate hunger with quality and healthy foods.

Equal access to food for all

This target aims to eliminate hunger and ensure that all people, especially the most vulnerable, have access to nutritious and sufficient food year-round. It involves addressing issues of food affordability, distribution, and accessibility, particularly in marginalized or food-insecure communities.
